PROGRAMS OFFERED

INFORMATION PORTAL FOR ADMISSIONS AT DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE


PhD(CS)

PhD program is the newest program on offer at the Department of Computer Science. It leads to PhD degree in Computer Science.




MS(CS)

MS(CS) program is offered every two years at the Department of Computer Science. It is a two-Year Degree Program (Master of Science in Computer Science) of 30 + credit hours spread over 4 semesters (2 semesters Course work plus thesis). It is Equivalent to MPhil (i.e. 18 years education)




MS(DS)new

MS(DS) program is offered every two years at the Department of Computer Science. It is a two-Year Degree Program (Master of Science in Data Science) of 30 + credit hours spread over 4 semesters (2 semesters Course work plus thesis). It is Equivalent to MPhil (i.e. 18 years education)




MCS

MCS program is offered as Morning and Evening programs at the Department of Computer Science. It is a two-Year Degree Program (Master of Computer Science â€“ Conversion Course) of 70+ credit hours spread over 4 semesters.




BS(CS)

BS(CS) program is the undergraduate program offered at the Department of Computer Science, offered in both Morning & Evening Sessions. It is a four-year Degree Program (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science) of 130 credit hours spread over 8 semesters.
